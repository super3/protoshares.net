What is a DAC?

  • Distributed

    With no central point of failure, and no human errors or misconduct, DACS provide a unique way to incentivize investors and new businesses.

  • Autonomous

    DAC services run autonomously in a decentralized network, utilizing block-chain technology to manage their affairs.

  • Community

    DACs are service-based entities. Funded by people, they generate profits for their backers from the services they provide.
